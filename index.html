About Batman

The Legend of Gotham

Batman, also known as Bruce Wayne, is a billionaire by day and a vigilante by night. Driven by the tragedy of his parents' murder, he dons the cape and cowl to protect Gotham City from crime and corruption.

With no superpowers, Batman relies on his intellect, physical prowess, and an arsenal of high-tech gadgets to fight villains like the Joker, Penguin, and Bane.
